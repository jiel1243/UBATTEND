Mission

The University of Batangas commits itself to provide progressive and quality education through its excellent academic programs, relevant research endeavors, and responsive community service initiatives, producing competent and ethical professionals who contribute to national development.

Vision

The University of Batangas envisions itself as a premier university of excellence, innovation, and social transformation in the CALABARZON region, recognized nationally and internationally for its outstanding academic programs, research contributions, and community engagement.

Core Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Integrity and Ethics
  • Innovation and Research
  • Social Responsibility
  • Student-Centered Learning
